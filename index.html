About Cafe Maya

Where Filipino hospitality meets exceptional coffee craftsmanship

Premium Coffee

We source the finest beans from Mt. Apo and craft each cup with passion and precision. Our signature Maya blend celebrates the rich coffee heritage of the Philippines.

Filipino Heritage

Located in the historic district of Jaro, we honor Filipino coffee traditions while embracing modern brewing techniques. Every cup tells a story of our cultural pride.

Community Hub

More than just a café, we're a gathering place for the Jaro community. From students to professionals, everyone finds their perfect spot at Cafe Maya.
